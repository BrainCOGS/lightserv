Brain Registration and Histology Core Facility Gallery

Transsynaptic viral tracing

The original use case of the Core Facility. Pisano et al. (in prep.) used light sheet imaging to map anatomical pathways between the cerebellum and sensorimotor, associative, and modulatory regions of the forebrain. Shown above is a three dimensional visualization of cell centers (light green) that were traced using the anterograde transsynaptic tracer herpes simplex virus type 1 (H129) and the retrograde tracer pseudorabies virus (Bartha). Also shown are cerebellar subregions from the Princeton Mouse Brain Atlas (PMA), a modified version of the Allen Mouse Brain Atlas (AMBA). The PMA extends the AMBA to include the posterior cerebellum, and it improves registration accuracy for light sheet images.

Technical specs:
Species: Mouse
Clearing protocol: iDISCO+ (with immunostaining)
Imaging resolution: 4x
Imaging modes: Registration (488 nm), Cell detection (647 nm)
Cell detection method: https://github.com/PrincetonUniversity/3dunet
Visualization: neuroglancer

c-Fos thalamic expression

Verpeut et al. (in prep.) used light sheet imaging to trace c-Fos expression in thalamic subregions after a Y-maze reversal task. Shown above is a horizontal slice through the brain of a mouse subject showing the light sheet data at cellular resolution in inverse color scale (gray background), selected regions of the Princeton Mouse Brain Atlas (colored regions), and detected cell centers (yellow circles) from Clearmap cell detection algorithm.

The visualization is shown at the resolution of the raw data (i.e. highest resolution possible in this experiment), and the brain atlas was transformed to the raw data coordinate framework for reference. On the right hand side are the anatomical labels for the selected brain regions. Clearmap is well suited for cell detection at this resolution (1.3x), and detects cells over a wide dynamic range. For example, the large, visibly obvious cells and the very faint, less obvious cells are simultaneously detected in a single run over the entire brain.

Technical specs:
Species: Mouse
Clearing protocol: iDISCO+ (with immunostaining)
Imaging resolution: 1.3x
Imaging modes: Registration (488 nm) - not shown, Cell detection (647 nm) - shown above
Cell detection method: https://github.com/PrincetonUniversity/ClearMapCluster
Visualization: neuroglancer
